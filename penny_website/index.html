“Penny” Hardaway: Skill, flair, and basketball IQ.

Anfernee Deon “Penny” Hardaway became one of the most exciting guards of the 90s, pairing size and playmaking with a calm Memphis swagger — and later taking that mindset to the sidelines as a head coach.

Career Timeline

Orlando Magic Era

Drafted in 1993, Penny quickly became one of the league’s brightest stars, forming a dynamic duo with Shaquille O’Neal.

Veteran Presence

A savvy playmaker with the Suns, Knicks, and Heat, proving his basketball IQ remained elite.

Signature Shoes

Select a shoe to see details. Only one shoe is shown at a time.

Air Foamposite One

The Nike Air Foamposite One "Royal", originally released in 1997 for $180, revolutionized basketball footwear with its molded polyurethane shell, designed by Eric Avar. Famously adopted by Penny Hardaway, this "Dark Neon Royal" shoe was known for its futuristic, beetle-shell look, full-length Zoom Air cushioning, and carbon fiber shanks.
